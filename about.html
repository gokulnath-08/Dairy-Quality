About Our Mini Project

Our mini project, IoT-Based Colorimetric Techniques and pH-Sensitive Packaging Solutions, leverages advanced sensor technology to monitor food quality in real time. The system integrates colorimetric sensors, which detect pH variations that indicate spoilage, ensuring enhanced food safety.

By connecting these sensors to an IoT-enabled platform, businesses can remotely track food conditions, improve inventory management, and build consumer trust through transparency. This innovative solution is particularly effective for dairy products and can be extended to other perishable goods, benefiting both industries and consumers alike.
